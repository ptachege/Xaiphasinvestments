Who are We

Xaiphas investments is an online clothing drop shipping company. Drop shipping is a form of retail business wherein the seller accepts customer orders but does not keep goods sold in stock. Instead, in a form of supply chain management, it transfers the orders and their shipment details to either the manufacturer, a wholesaler, another retailer, or a fulfillment house, which then ships the goods directly to the customer. As such, the retailer is responsible for marketing and selling a product, but has little or no control over product quality, storage, inventory management, or shipping.


Xaiphas investments is an enterprise made of competitive digital marketers and online researchers who update, market and sell new fashion trends on related websites. They cover a wide range of options from all genders to occasional fashions. Xaiphas investments has also a window of financial investment in that for every amount you deposit/ invest in their site on the due date an interest of 10% on the initial amount is added on your withdrawal. For example, an investor invested Sh2000 in a time span of a week i.e. 10% / 100% * 2000 = 200 after his total amount will be 2000+200 = 2200. Xaiphas investments cuts across all clothing websites and updates its fashion trends weekly basis. At the moment we deal with website to website drop shipping as we grow our individual customer service which in a shorter while we will deliver the best customer service.
